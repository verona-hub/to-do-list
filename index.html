How to use

Add a task

Click inside the yellow box and add text.

Once done, press Enter or click on the + button.

Complete a task

Hover over the task and click the square box.

Edit a task

Hover over the task and click the pencil icon.

You can now edit the task.

Once done, press Enter or click on the pencil icon again.

Remove a task

Hover over the task and click the recycle bin.
